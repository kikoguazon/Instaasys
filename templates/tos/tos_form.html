How TOS Fits the Workflow

INSTAASYS uses one connected pipeline. The TOS is the blueprint that tells the assessment generator how many items each topic gets and how to spread them across Bloom's levels.

  1. Presentations — the AI reads your syllabus + CILOs and produces slide content per week. This becomes the knowledge base for the course.
  2. TOS (this page) — you specify exam type, items, topics, and teaching hours. The AI builds the item distribution table.
  3. Assessment — the quiz/exam generator pulls topic content from your presentations and follows the TOS distribution to write actual questions.
How to Generate a TOS
  1. Fill in Step 1 — Exam Details. Choose the exam type (Prelim, Midterm, Finals) and enter the total number of items the exam will contain.
  2. Load topics in Step 2. Click Load from Syllabus to auto-fill topics from your course's weekly plan, then select which weeks to include. Or click Add Topic to add them manually.
  3. Set teaching hours per topic. Hours determine how many items each topic gets (proportional weighting).
  4. Click ✦ Generate TOS. The AI distributes items across Bloom's levels (Remember → Create) and outputs an Excel file.
Requirements Before Generating
  • Exam type — selected from the dropdown.
  • Total items — at least 10. Common values: 30, 50, 60, 100.
  • At least one topic — ideally 3–8 topics for a realistic spread.
  • Teaching hours per topic — whole numbers 1–99. More hours = more items.
What the AI Will Do
  • Allocate items proportionally by teaching hours.
  • Distribute items across Bloom's six levels: Remember, Understand, Apply, Analyze, Evaluate, Create.
  • Ensure at least 30% of items target higher-order thinking (Analyze / Evaluate / Create).
  • Number the items per topic so totals match your exam length.
  • Output a formatted .xlsx file ready to submit.
