How it works
  1. Select the weeks you want to generate presentations for.
  2. The AI analyses your CILOs and Topics for each selected week.
  3. A combined presentation is built and added to your lesson library.
  4. Download, view, and present directly from your browser.
What happens next

Generated presentations don't just sit in your lesson library — they become the knowledge base for the rest of INSTAASYS:

  • The AI captures topics, key points, and CILOs into the slide JSON.
  • When you build a TOS, you list the same topics — the TOS sets how many items each topic gets and the Bloom distribution.
  • When you generate an Assessment, the quiz/exam generator pulls slide content from these presentations and follows the TOS to write actual questions.
Requirements
  • Course must have an uploaded syllabus with extracted weekly plan.
  • Each selected week needs topics and CILOs in the syllabus.
  • At least one configured AI provider key (Groq, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google).
  • Pick a slide count (8–14) and a content depth (Overview / Detailed).
  • Generate weeks before creating their TOS or assessment for best AI grounding.
