About INSTAASYS

AI-Assisted Instructor's Academic Assistant System — Revolutionizing Education Through Intelligent Automation

Our Mission

INSTAASYS is designed to create an AI-powered system that lightens the load for instructors. By using artificial intelligence, the system automates the creation of PowerPoint presentations aligned with the course syllabus, as well as preparing Tables of Specifications (TOS). It also generates exam questions, quizzes, and oral assessments based on set criteria, helping instructors save time and effort on these routine tasks.

We believe that teachers should spend more time inspiring students and less time on administrative paperwork. Our AI-powered platform streamlines the entire academic workflow, from lesson planning to grade management.

Complete System Features

INSTAASYS provides a comprehensive suite of AI-powered tools for modern educators:

AI PowerPoint Generation

Automatically creates polished presentations aligned with your syllabus and weekly course plan.

Table of Specifications

Generates exam blueprints (TOS) for midterms and finals with proper cognitive level distribution.

Question Generation

Creates multiple-choice, essay, and oral assessment questions based on Bloom's taxonomy.

e-Class Record System

Manages student assessments including quizzes, projects, midterm, and final scores with CHED-standard weighting.

Attendance Management

Integrated attendance system with automated reports and participation analytics.

Student Portal

Students can view grades, attendance records, and track academic progress in real-time.

How It Works

INSTAASYS integrates cutting-edge AI technology to transform a simple syllabus into a complete academic workflow in 4 simple steps, streamlining everything for modern educators.

1
Upload Your Syllabus

Provide your existing .docx syllabus. The system will parse reading assignments, topics, objectives, and internal course structures automatically.

2
AI Generates Materials

In minutes, our core AI engines dynamically construct polished slide presentations (PPT), daily lesson plans, examination question banks, and exhaustive Tables of Specifications (TOS).

3
Review & Conduct Classes

Deliver high-quality lessons natively via PowerPoint exports. Execute oral assessments or deploy digital quizzes directly from your instructor portal.

4
Automated Grading & Tracking

Track attendance seamlessly and let the robust e-Class Record strictly follow compliance standards (e.g., CHED) for secure, real-time grade calculations.
